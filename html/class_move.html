Detailed Description

Every move usuable by a player, eidos, or monster is handled here. Must be associated with a Combatant Sub-Class.

Combatants have associated Moves that have a function to validate casting of the spell, and execution of the actual spell. every instance of a Move is aware of the combatant for the sake of being able to figure out who the target of x Combatant is, and for applying status effects.
